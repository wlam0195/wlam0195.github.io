Roger Savon Court, 40

from Cuba to USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

To cross into the United States, Court traveled through Colombia, South America and Guatemala. He wanted to work honestly in America. In his bag, he brought a sweater, two caps, cigars, a wallet, toilet paper, a big shell tailsman to guide him on the journey, a headdress, a fanny pack, a necklace, a document holder, a small Virgin Mary statue, a cell phone, hair gel and a detergent oil for the skin.

Where are they now?

As of November 20202, Court's current whereabouts are unknown.
