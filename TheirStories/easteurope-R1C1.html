Abraham Burstein

from Russia to New York, USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

In 1921, Abraham Burstein arrived to New York as a Russian Jewish immigrant. He didn't bring much, as all of his belongings included a change of underwear and two books.

Where are they now?

As of 2020, Burstein's whereabouts are unknown.
