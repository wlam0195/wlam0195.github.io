Their Stories

Their Journey

In 1985, Jacqui and her family left South Africa for the United States to avoid the Apartheid system. They took an airplane to their new home, but they had to secretly hide their money in their belongings to get by. In this situation, Jacqui brought chocolate boxes stuffed with cash.

Where are they now?

Jacqui completely assimilated into American culture. She attended college at UC Santa Barbara, where she went on to become a teacher. She married her high school sweetheart and now has two kids, aged 17 and 20.
