Chi Yau Lam, 25

from Guangdong, China to USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

Chi Yau Lam was born on October 25, 1963 in Guangdong, China. In 1980's, Lam immigrated to the United States from Hong Kong with his fiance's wedding dress, immigration papers, and less than $100 in his pocket. He would be the first in his family to immigrate to the United States. Prior to his departure, Lam attended attending technical school for print production in Hong Kong.


Where are they now?

After joining his now-wife in America, Lam opened a print shop, Candid Bindery Inc. He would go on to have two daughters, who he claims are his pride and joy. His eldest is an employment lawyer while the youngest is studying Graphic Design at St. John's University.

Source: Winnie Lam, daughter (2020)
