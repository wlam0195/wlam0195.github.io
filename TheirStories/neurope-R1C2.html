Delia O’Toole, 17

from County Galway to Boston, Massuchusetts

Their Stories

Their Journey

Delia O’Toole arrived in the Boston Harbor on May 5th, 1903, where she met up with her older sister, Mary. She didn't bring much, but she did bring the names of her dead neighbors and the ballads to their memories. Like most immigrants that followed their family to the USA, O'Toole washed floors, cooked, and cared for others children. She sent her wages home in hopes of getting the rest of her family to America.

Where are they now?

After settling in Portland, Maine, Delia married longshoreman John Curran in St. Dominic's Church. They would go on to have four children.
