Margaret Lehan

from Italy to USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

Margaret Lehan immigrated to the United States with her mother in 1911. They brought a variety of items: all of their clothes, and the food that my grandmother liked: all kinds of cheese and sausage.

Where are they now?

As of 2020, Lehan's whereabouts are unknown.
