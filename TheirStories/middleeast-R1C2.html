Mohammed Dowlati

from Afghanistan to USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

Dowlati, his wife and two children at the time (he now has three) came to the U.S. four years ago on special immigrant visas (SIVs). He hoped to find a job with his experience in computers, but his status and lack of connections left him without much hope.

Where are they now?

Currently, Dowlati works at Taste of Persia food truck in downtown Washington D.C. Dowlati lives with his family at their apartment in Riverdale, MD. Two of his children came over from Afghanistan. His third was born here.
