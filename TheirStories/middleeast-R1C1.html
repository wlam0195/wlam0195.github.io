Farhat Popal, 3 Months

from Afghanistan to USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

When Farhat Popal was three months old, her parents left Afghanistan with their student visas at hand, fleeing horror of the Soviet Union invasion in 1979. They lost their home, their livelihoods, precious photos, and family dog. Prior to immigration, her mom was a college-educated reporter and children's magazine editor.

Where are they now?

The Popal family became American citizens in 2004, twelve years after they immigrated to the United States.
