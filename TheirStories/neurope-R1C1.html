Ellen C. O'Rourke, 10

from Leitrim, Ireland to Hamden, Connecticut

Their Stories

Their Journey

Ellen Canning O'Rourke immigrated to America with her family in 1930. O'Rourke worked as a "domestic live-in". She "viewed coming to America to work as a gift". She didn't bring much, but she did bring the names of her dead neighbors and the ballads to their memories.


Where are they now?

There isn't much information about her career and life after arrival. O'Rouke passed away on December 16, 2011 at the age of 101.
