from Cuba to California, USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

Nasreen was born and raised in Kabul, Afghanistan. She left Afghanistan when she was 16 years old following the Russian invasion of 1979. During the Russian-Afghan War of 1979-1989, Nasreen’s family was threatened and was severely affected because of their social position.

Where are they now?

Currently, Nasreen lives in the Washington, DC metro area and works as a teacher.
