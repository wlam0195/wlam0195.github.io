Robert, 19

from Kenya to USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

In 1988, Robert left the country for a better life. His brother immigrated to Miami, Flordia years prior, so he felt no hesitation or fear arriving.

Where are they now?

Through Robert's brother and a travel agent, he was able to secure a job interview and quickly adjust to the different political climate. He enrolled in a trade school at an adult education center, where he became a nursing assistant.
