Arthur Kinyanjui, 26

from Kenya to California, USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

Kinyanjui grew up in a close-knitted community. He was employed at a teacher when he won the green card lottery, which guaranteed clearance, medical records, immunization, etc. With $2,301.54, just enough for a plane ticket, Kinyanjui and his fiance left for America.

Where are they now?

Kinyanjui found employmnent as a teller at Wells Fargo when he first arrived. He now shortly worked as a student attendnat at Palo Alto High School before becoming a certified history teacher at Henry M. Gunn High School.
