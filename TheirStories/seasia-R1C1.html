Their Journey

In 2010, Tulip Nandu left India with a FI and HI-B vias to pursue a second master degree in molecular biology and genetics at Indiana University. He wanted a "American university life" and experience the different education system.


Where are they now?

Nandu was hired at the UT Southerwestern Medical Center in Dallas. He has been working as a acomputational biologist since 2013. He is currently pursuing a MBA from the University of Texas at Dallas.
