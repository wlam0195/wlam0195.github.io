Sandy Pan

from China to USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

In 1996, Pan immigrated to New York City from China with only two piece of luggage and one-thousand dollars. Right off the start, she focused on helping those in the Asian American community. Her first job was working at an assistant team leader at a prevented program that supported urban families.


Where are they now?

As of 2018, Pan works as a Youth Services Coordinator at Charles B. Wang Community Center. Her goal is to "educate people in the mainstream community about what Asian Americans are going through and get them the right resources".
