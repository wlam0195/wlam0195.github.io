Carmel Mahon

from Glasgow, Ireland to New York, USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

In 1990s, Carmel Mahon immigrated to the United States without a clear plan for her future. She brought around $500—$800 as well as two suitcases. Her great-uncle, George Toner Bradley, immigrated from Glasgow in 1929, but disappeared after arriving to Ellis Island.

Where are they now?

As of 2018, Mahon has been working on the Queens Irish Oral History Project with Queens College to preserve the stories of fellow disappearing Irish immigrants.
