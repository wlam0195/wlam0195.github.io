Their Journey

In 1961, Vavilala had earned two electrical engineering degrees and married a chemist of the highest academic recognition in 1941. Similar to many other Americans, he packed ha single suticase, containing only his father-in-law's PhD thesis, clothes, and immigration papers to the US.


Where are they now?

As of October 2015, Vavilala has been working as a software architect.
