Ximena, 6

from Lima, Peru to Florida, USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

Ximena grew up in Lima, Peru. After her younger brother's death, her family separated. Ximena and her mom left for Peru, but returned several months later to rework their marriage. They offically moved to Miami, Florida after Ximena's father was offered a position at a seafood distribution company in Florida.


Where are they now?

After her father was diagnosed with Leukemia and lost his job, his work visa shortly expired. Ximena and her family were without papers, but secured a visa through DACA, becoming a teacher at Montessori school.
