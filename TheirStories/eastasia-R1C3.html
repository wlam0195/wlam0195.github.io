Zhao Lu

Their Stories

Their Journey

In the 1990s, Zhao Lu's siblings decided to immigrate to the United States for a better life. Prior to his departure, he worked as a federal inventory control specialist and clerk at the post office. Knowing the difficulties ahead, he brought a suitcase full of cook books to help his brother as a fry cook in the kitchens.


Where are they now?

As of 2020, Lu has retired in Flushing with his wife. His wife and five daughters joined him in the United States shortly after his arrival. He enjoys learning how to work with new technology and travel throughout the world.
