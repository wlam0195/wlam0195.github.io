Michela Sanchez, 4

from Lima, Peru to Salt Lake City, Utah

Their Stories

Their Journey

Sanchez's parents decided to move to the United States when Michela was four years old. Her dad, a personal contractor, worked hard to save enough money to fund their trip.


Where are they now?

Sanchez's family saved up enough money to move to Suncrest, a nicer area of their town. They are proud that they can buy designer clothes, take vacations, cars and develop their dream house in America. Although she does not remember much, Sanchez remembers the Peruvian culture that her family brought with them to America.
