Winnie Hiuh

from Nairobi, Kenya to San Francisco, USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

In September of 1997, Hiuhu left Nairobi for the United States with a small black suitcase with all of her belongings. She had just graduated Kenyatta University years prior, graduating with a BFA in Literature. She became a babysitter, married Kimani, and went to community college

Where are they now?

After having two daughters, she parted ways with Kimani. She became an executive assistant in Oakland California, where she has been working ever since. Her eldest daughter is now attending a New York university and her youngest is in high school.
