Carlos Gomez

from Guatemala to Miami, USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

Carlos Gomez, 34. A decade ago, Carlos immigrated to the United States. He stayed in Miami for around ten years. He brought a shirt, a pair of scissors, a pair of pants, razor blades, pill, shampoo, deodorant, a can of coke and a t-shirt.

Where are they now?

Gomez was deported back to Guatemala in August 2015. As of November 2020, his current residence is unknown.
