Cristina Rodriguez, 10

from Cuba to California, USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

Cristina Rodriguez immigrated to America from Cuba around 1964 because of Fidel Castro. Her family worked for the government, but her father was imprisoned because of Fidel Castro. Her parents wanted her to come to America before something happened to the her and her siblings. She could only bring a small bag containing an extra pair of clothes, maybe some documentation, a baby picture of herself, and a few other possessions.

Where are they now?

Rodriguez currently lives in Houston, Texas. She worked ‘basic jobs’ her whole life. She never went to college, becoming a mother at sixteen.
