Fortunata Ule

from Sicily, Italy to USA

Their Stories

Their Journey

In 1931, Fortunata, two month baby, one year child, and nine years old daughter journeyed from Italy to America. They were received by her husband, Antonio, in none of them spoke English. Fortunata had hid a bottle of champagne in the baby's diaper.

Where are they now?

Once reunited with her husband, the family boarded a train and rode it across the country to San Bernardino, California. Further information is unknown, aside from that she would have a grandchild.
