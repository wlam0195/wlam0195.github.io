‘Rabbit’ is a serif font that takes influence from comic and calligraphic elements. As the name suggests, the font draws inspiration from the bouncing and hopping movement of rabbits. The font has bulbous terminals that resembles the animal’s ears. The thickness of the font is the heaviest on the stem, reaching its thinnest point at the shoulders. The ends of the letters are sharply curved to provide contrast and further active eye movement. Composed entirely of ovals, 'Rabbit' is best used as a playful display font.
